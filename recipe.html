Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

15 mins
4 servings
280 kcal

Description

This vibrant Mediterranean chickpea salad is packed with fresh vegetables, herbs, and a zesty lemon dressing. It's perfect for meal prep, picnics, or a quick healthy lunch. High in fiber and plant-based protein, this salad will keep you satisfied for hours.

Nutrition Highlights

High Fiber
Plant Protein
Low GI
Vitamin C

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. 1 In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olives, and parsley.
  2. 2 In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. 3 Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. 4 If using, sprinkle feta cheese over the top.
  5. 5 Let the salad sit for 10 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld.
  6. 6 Serve chilled or at room temperature. Stores well in refrigerator for up to 3 days.
